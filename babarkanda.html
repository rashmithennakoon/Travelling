Bambarakanda Falls







Location




Horton Plains, Nuwara Eliya District






Height




With a height of approximately 263 meters, Bambarakanda is the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka






Scenic Surroundings




Surrounded by picturesque tea plantations and misty hills, Bambarakanda is a breathtaking sight.
