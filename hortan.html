Horton Plains National Park







Location




Situated in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, near Nuwara Eliya






Unique Features




While not a typical rainforest, Horton Plains is an important protected area with unique grasslands and cloud forests. It is home to a variety of wildlife, including the elusive Horton Plains slender loris and sambar deer






World's End




A famous attraction within the park is "World's End," a sheer cliff that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
