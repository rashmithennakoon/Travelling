Anuradhapura








Location




North Central Province.






Sacred City




Anuradhapura was the first capital of ancient Sri Lanka and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Key attractions include the sacred Bodhi tree, Ruwanwelisaya stupa, and Abhayagiri Monastery.
