Gampaha Botanical Garden (Henarathgoda Botanical Garden)












Location




Gampaha, Western Province.






Specialization




Established in 1876, it is known for its historic rubber tree, the first rubber tree planted in Sri Lanka. The gardens also feature a spice garden, an ornamental garden, and a fernery.
