Kanneliya-Dediyagala-Nakiyadeniya (KDN) Forest Complex



















Location




Located in the Galle District, this forest complex is another biodiversity hotspot in Sri Lanka.






Biodiversity




Known for its rich flora and fauna, the KDN Forest Complex is home to a variety of endemic species, including the green pit viper and the Kanneliya rocket frog.






Importance




It is recognized for its conservation value and is part of Sri Lanka's efforts to preserve its natural heritage.
