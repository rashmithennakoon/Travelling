Knuckles Mountain Range







Location




The Knuckles Mountain Range, also known as the Knuckles Massif or Dumbara Kanduvetiya, is located in the central part of Sri Lanka






Biodiversity




It is known for its diverse flora and fauna, including cloud forests, grasslands, and unique geological formations. The area is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.






Hiking and Trekking




The Knuckles Range is popular among hikers and trekkers for its scenic trails and panoramic views. The challenging terrain offers various trekking opportunities.
