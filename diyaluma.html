Diyaluma Falls







Location




Koslanda, Badulla District






Height




Diyaluma is the second-highest waterfall in Sri Lanka, cascading down a series of natural rock terraces






Natural Pools




The waterfall forms several natural pools, making it a popular destination for swimming and trekking.
